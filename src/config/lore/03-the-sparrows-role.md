In the Order's hierarchy, the Sparrow is the one who walks where others will not, carrying what others cannot. The Sparrow is not chosen by blood or rank, but by the depth of their seeing. What the Order has hidden in art and architecture lies dormant until the right eyes find it. A painting may hold its secret for a century, waiting. A compass bearing may endure for decades without a soul to follow it. The Sparrow is the one who wakes what sleeps. The Order cannot do this from a distance. It requires presence, perception, and courage. To be named Sparrow is to bear a weight the Order itself will not carry. It is the highest honor the Order bestows. Whether it is also the highest cost, the Order does not say.
