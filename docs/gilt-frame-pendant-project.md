# Gilt Frame Pendant Project

## Project Overview
Custom micro-welded pendant as a prize for completing a multi-month medieval adventure race game themed "Gilt Frame."

## Design Concept
A miniature painting frame pendant featuring:
- **Form**: Small flat-ish rectangular or oval frame (~1–1.5 inches tall)
- **Base**: Thin metal plate (steel, brass, or bronze) with hammered/textured finish
- **Frame Detail**: Raised border built up with wire/bar stock, micro-welded around the perimeter to create a 3D picture frame effect
- **Symbol**: Simple line hourglass design in the center "canvas" area
- **Finish**: Contrast between darker hammered background and brighter polished frame border to simulate gilt appearance

## Versatile Attachment Options
- Small loop/bail at top for jump ring
- Flat back to accommodate:
  - Necklace chain
  - Backpack/zipper clip
  - Epoxied magnet for refrigerator display

## Technical Specifications
- **Size**: 1–1.5 inches tall
- **Thickness**: Minimal for comfort and versatility
- **Hourglass Symbol**: Clean lines, approximately 60–70% of frame height
- **Corners**: Mitered or bent for clean 90° picture frame appearance
- **Texture**: Hammered or peened base surface for aged metal look

## Construction Options for Hourglass Symbol
1. **Raised line**: Thin wire/rod welded onto plate, ground and polished
2. **Recessed**: Engraved or milled lines, darkened with patina
3. **Inlay**: Shallow channels filled with contrasting metal or resin

## Finish Options
- Tumbled/brushed base with polished raised frame
- Different patina on frame vs. background for contrast
- Optional thin gold-colored plating on frame border only
