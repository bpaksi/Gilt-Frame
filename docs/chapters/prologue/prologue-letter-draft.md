# Prologue Letter — Draft v1

**Hidden acrostic:** I SEE CLEARLY (first letter of each paragraph in the body)
**Signed by:** The Registrar
**Format:** FedEx ivory cardstock, single-sided, Marker glyph centered at top
**Envelope:** Marker glyph top-left, no return address, recipient address centered

---

[Marker glyph centered]

Candidate,

In the records of the Order of the Gilt Frame, your name has been inscribed. This was not done lightly, nor recently.

Seldom does the Council reach such swift consensus on a candidate. Your attention was noted long before this letter was sealed.

Every generation, we identify those who possess a rare gift — the ability to see what others overlook. To notice what hides in plain sight.

Enclosed within these words is everything you need to begin. Read carefully.

Consider this your formal summons. The trials ahead will ask nothing of strength or speed. Only of sight.

Look not for what is obvious. The Order has never valued the obvious.

Each threshold you cross will reveal another. The first awaits you now.

Accept what you find at giltframe.org, and the Order will know you have answered.

Refuse, and this letter is merely paper — nothing more.

Long have the frames watched. Long have they waited.

You have been chosen, Sparrow.

— The Registrar
The Order of the Gilt Frame
